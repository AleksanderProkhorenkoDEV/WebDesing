Instruments

01

High-Quality Chef's Knives: Knives are essential for precise ingredient preparation. Chefs often have a specialized collection of knives for different tasks.

02

Precision Tongs: Kitchen tongs are crucial for delicately and precisely handling ingredients, particularly in plating

03

Spatulas and Plating Rings: These utensils help shape and present dishes in an elegant and creative manner

04

Mandoline: A mandoline is a tool that allows for thin and uniform slicing of ingredients, which is useful for presentation and preparation.

05

Kitchen Thermometer: Michelin-star chefs use kitchen thermometers to monitor the cooking temperature of meats, fish, and other ingredients.

06

Graters and Microplanes: For grating and creating fine shavings of ingredients such as cheese, citrus, or spices.
