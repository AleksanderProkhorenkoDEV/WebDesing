Carmen Ruscatelle

In Spain, she holds the most Michelin stars for a chef, and she also has the highest rating (three suns) from the Campsa-Repsol Guide

Her cuisine is deeply rooted in Catalan tradition but incorporates a modern touch. Opening a restaurant in Japan and her subsequent travels there sparked her interest in some Japanese culinary techniques, which she adapted to Catalan cuisine and products.

She is passionate about making her cuisine healthy and focuses on seasonal ingredients from her region. She often incorporates literary references into her culinary creations and has shared her knowledge through publications for the general public.

Kitchen

Cooking is much more than a simple act of preparing food. It is an exciting journey through the flavors, aromas and textures of different culinary traditions. With an unwavering passion for gastronomic art, at the Arriaga Restaurant in Granada we immerse ourselves in the essence of each ingredient, exploring its potential and combining it with mastery to create dishes that are authentic edible works of art.

Excite

The act of cooking is a powerful catalyst capable of exciting your senses and unleashing an unmatched passion. Each dish we serve is an invitation to an exciting culinary journey that will stimulate your deepest feelings and trigger an explosion of flavors and emotions.

Unite

The dining room of our restaurant is the meeting point where the passion for culinary art and the desire to please our guests converge. Each dish we create is an expression of this union, matching flavors, aromas and textures in harmony to create a unique gastronomic experience.

Our History

In this house full of tradition and history, the famous literary gathering of 'Luxury Restaurant' convened between 1915 and 1929. It was a momentous period when the most distinguished intellectuals, politicians, and artists of the time converged. Notably, luminaries like Federico García Lorca, his brother Francisco, Manuel de Falla, and a very young Andrés Segovia, among others, were active participants.

Undoubtedly, this era marked an unparalleled generation of creativity and intellect.

Whiting in brine

The exquisite dish 'Whiting in Brine' is an ode to sophistication and simplicity in a single bite. It begins with the delicacy of whiting, whose flesh is bathed in a carefully prepared brine solution.
