Frequently asked questions

How can I contact the support team for assistance with a specific route or application?

You can reach our dedicated support team by sending an email to support@cosmosexplore.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours to address your queries and provide assistance.

Are the conference tickets refundable if I'm unable to attend the event?

Unfortunately, conference tickets are non-refundable. However, we do understand that unexpected situations may arise. Please contact our support team at least 72 hours before the event, and we'll do our best to assist you with alternative arrangements.

Question: Can I suggest a new astronomical route to be featured on the website?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new astronomical routes. Please navigate to the "Suggest a Route" section on our website, fill out the form with details about the proposed route, and our team will review your submission.

What security measures are in place for online ticket purchases on your website?

We prioritize the security of your online transactions. Our website employs industry-standard encryption protocols to safeguard your payment information. Additionally, we partner with trusted payment gateways to ensure a secure and seamless purchasing experience.

Question: Is there a mobile app available for accessing the educational applications on the go?

Yes, we offer a mobile app for both Android and iOS devices. You can download it from the respective app stores. The app provides convenient access to our educational applications, making it easy for users to explore astronomy lessons anytime, anywhere.
